Registration

 

1 Registration Information

Online Registration: April 2nd, 2018 - October 15th, 2018

On-site Registration: October 18th, 2018, 13: 00-17: 00

Registration Fee: This conference does not charge any registration fee.

 

2 Conference Instructions

1, Registration: Log in to the website for “Environmental Conference for Doctoral Students” (http://ndac.env.tsinghua.edu.cn) for online registration. You may also register onsite and collect related materials at 13: 00-17: 00 on October 18th, 1st floor of School of Environment, Tsinghua University.

 

2, All participants should bring and post their own posters in the corresponding location according to the number issued by the organizing committee.

 

3, The time for report at each venue is 10-15 minutes with 5-minute Q&A section. It is recommended that all participants make brief introduction about themselves and their own group when giving reports.

 

4, During the conference, all participants are required to abide by the schedules and complete the oral report, while the venue coordinators will record their performance. In principle, the Organizing Committee only issues certification to those participants who have completed their oral reports.

 

5, After the closing ceremony, participants with certification (which demonstrates their completion of conference) could contact the organizing committee for reimbursement of expenses. For any special circumstances, please contact the organizing committee in advance.

 

6, Doctoral students that will give oral reports should copy their documents to the computer (used for projection and demonstration) 15 minutes before the sub-conference starts. Chairmen, venue coordinators and other staff should arrive at their respective position in advance and prepare ahead of schedule.

 

7, Oral reports proceed according to the predetermined order. If any reporter does not show up for the report, then his/her report will be skipped and the next participant should continue to report.

 

8, The scoring process should be fair and open, responsible to the reporters; judges should submit the scoring sheets to the venue coordinators for follow-up arrangements.

 

9, Please come and find a seat in the conference venue in advance and follow the instructions of staff members. Do not bring snacks or drinks into the venue. Smoking is strictly prohibited in the building.

 

10, The “Environmental Conference for Doctoral Students” will be video-recording the keynote speeches and some oral reports at the sub-venues. Please turn off or mute your mobile phone; please do not chat or walk around; during the reporting period, if you need to enter or leave the venue, please keep quiet.

 

11, Please cherish this opportunity to exchange ideas, listen attentively, think hard and raise questions so as to achieve the purpose of expanding academic horizon, stimulating creative inspiration, inspiring scientific thinking and promoting academic friendship.

 

12, Meals and school visits: the route is to be determined, please pay attention to any updates on the website.

 

13, If you need to leave in advance or you have any further inquiries, please contact the organizing committee. Contacts: Ms. Fang Kuo (13131488864), Mr. Zhang Shuo (18201063906).
