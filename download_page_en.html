The Eleventh National Conference of Doctoral Students (2017)

Venue: School of Environment, Tsinghua University

Subject: 2017 National Conference of Doctoral Students on New Theories and New Technology of Environmental Science and Engineering

 

Sub-forum themes

01 Environmental Chemistry and Materials

02 Water Pollution Control and Recycling ---- Session A

03 Environmental Economics, Management and Policy

04 Solid Waste Pollution Control and Recycling ---- Session A

05 Water Pollution Control and Recycling ---- Session B

06 Environmental and Ecological Wellness

07 Air Pollution Control

08 Water Pollution Control and Recycling ---- Session C

09 Energy and Climate Change

10 Water Pollution Control and Recycling ---- Session D

11 Solid Waste Pollution Control and Recycling ---- Session B

12 Sub-Forum: Reports and presentations in English

 

Participating Universities

Tsinghua University, Tongji University, Xi’an University of Architecture and Technology, Fudan University, Xi’an Jiaotong University, Peking University, Nankai University, Jilin University, Wuhan University, Dalian University of Technology, China University of Geosciences (Beijing), China University of Geosciences (Wuhan) , Lanzhou University, Southeast University, Chang’an University, Nanjing University, Institute of Urban Environment, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing University of Chemical Technology, Northeast Institute of Geography and Agroecology Chinese Academy of Sciences, Chongqing University, South China University of Technology, Ocean University of China and etc., a total of 47 universities and research institutions.

 

The number of participants

135 participating doctoral students; over 20 teachers (including Hao Jiming, Wu Ye, Huang Xia, Lu Xi, Liang Peng, Wu Jing, Zhou Xiaohong, Chen Chao, Zeng Xianlai, Wang Bin, Lu Yun, Dong Xin, Ni Jinren, Liu Huan, Zhang Xiaoyuan, etc.)

 

The number of reports

A total of 88 oral reports were published in 12 sub-venues and 80 excellent posters were displayed. At the opening ceremony, Academician Yue Guojun and Qu Jiuhui delivered keynote speeches.

 

Participating guests

Academician Yue Guojun, State Development and Investment Corporation, delivered a keynote speech;

Academician Qu Jiuhui, Tsinghua University, delivered a keynote speech;

Professor Yao Qiang, Dean of Graduate School, Tsinghua University, attended the opening ceremony and academic exchange;

Professor Liu Yi, Secretary of the Party Committee of School of Environment, Tsinghua University, attended the opening ceremony and academic exchange;

Ms. Dai Weitong, president of Novozymes Asia Pacific, attended the opening ceremony and academic exchange;

Professor Huang Xia, Tsinghua University, Director of the State Key Laboratory, Chang Jiang Scholar, attended the opening ceremony and academic exchange;

Professor Wu Ye, Assistant Dean of School of Environment, Tsinghua University, winner of Outstanding Youth Science Foundation, attended the opening ceremony and academic exchange;

Associate Professor Lu Xi, School of Environment, Tsinghua University, The Recruitment Program of Global Experts, attended the closing ceremony and academic exchange.
